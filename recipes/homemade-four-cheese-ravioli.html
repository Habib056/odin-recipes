Homemade Four Cheese Ravioli

Description

This recipe should help to be able to make a really delicious Homemade Four Cheese Ravioli. It includes much loved Italian ingredients like pesto-Alfredo, marinara, and of cheese (four cheese to be exact!).

Don't be intimated by the long list of ingredients. You probably already have most of them in your kitchen. If not, you can make a quick trip to the store or make a substitution.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Provolone Cheese
  • Dried Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Prepared Basil pesto sauce
  • Heavy cream
  • Marinara

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Make the filing
  3. Make the sauce
  4. Assemble the ravioli
  5. Cook and bake the ravioli
  6. Serve the ravioli
