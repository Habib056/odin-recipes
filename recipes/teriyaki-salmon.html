Teriyaki Salmom

Description

This Teriyaki Salmon dish is an easy to make delicious seafood dish. It only has a short list of ingredients and simple three steps to make.

Ingredients

  • Sesame oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Spices
  • Salmon

Steps

  1. Make the marinade on the stove, then set aside 1/4cup for basting.
  2. Marinate the salmon in zip-top bags in the refrigerator for up to two hours.
  3. Broil the salmon until it flakes easily, brushing with the reserved marinade as-needed.
